Privacy Policy for Stadium Energy Drinks

At Stadium, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy describes the types of personal information we may collect, how we use that information, and how we protect it.

Collection of Information

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and shipping address when you place an order on our website. We may also collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system to help us improve our website and provide better service.

Use of Information

We use your personal information to process your orders, communicate with you about your orders, and improve our products and services. We may also use your personal information to send you promotional materials and special offers that we think may be of interest to you. You may opt-out of receiving these communications at any time.

Protection of Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard encryption technology to protect it. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to process your orders and comply with legal requirements.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and personalize your experience on our website. Cookies are small files that are stored on your computer and help us understand how you use our website. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use some features of our website.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or legal requirements. Any changes to this policy will be posted on our website.
